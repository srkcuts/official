Behind The Camera & The Cut

A Tondiarpet-based photography, videography & editing studio built on one idea — every moment deserves a timeless story.

SRK Cuts Edit Zone captures & edits weddings, birthdays, baby showers, puberty functions and social content — end to end. From the shoot day to the final pendrive delivery, one team handles photography, videography, album design and post-production.

Based in Tondiarpet, Chennai, we work across the city for events, and remotely for content creators & businesses who need reels, YouTube edits and social media content.
